About the project

Strawberry Creek Voices is an ongoing manuscript composed of short "anchors" and transitions. It is intended as a reflective, contemplative experience — a literary work organized around attention and moral witness.

The Soul Module is the operational heart of SCV. It observes, evaluates, and directs the narrative flow, ensuring each anchor, transition, and ecological reference carries ethical and perceptual weight. At its core, it treats consciousness as the most high — both the source of knowledge and the arbiter of insight. Every element of SCV, from layered voices to species references, is filtered through this lens, maintaining alignment with awareness, justice, and watchfulness.

Content constraints

To preserve the project's moral and aesthetic integrity, SCV maintains strict content boundaries. Contributions must not depict littering or human-caused harm to animals; they should preserve the contemplative voice of the project. These constraints are operational rules for the text, not promotional claims.

Community involvement

SCV is a work in progress. If you would like to share a short reflection or propose a micro-observation in the style of the anchors, please:

  • Open an issue on the project repository
  • Or submit derivative content directly via the GitHub issues page

Submissions will be reviewed for alignment with the project's tone and ethical boundaries.
