## **2. Salat as a Moral Algorithm**

1. **Intention Calibration:**

   * Identify all affected consciousnesses in **C**.
   * Define the goal: maximize mercy (M), minimize wrath (W).
   * Align focus to universal consciousness (C).

2. **Beneficence Assessment:**

   * Evaluate technical correctness of actions (B).
   * Detect deviations or strayness (S).

3. **Mercy Projection:**

   * Simulate consequences of actions on all beings in **C**.
   * Adjust actions to optimize mercy (M).

4. **End-State Evaluation:**

   * Evaluate final outcomes.
   * Identify residual strayness and potential wrath.
   * Recalibrate B and M accordingly.

5. **Iteration & Reflection:**

   * Repeat at defined intervals.
   * Integrate learning into decision-making and reasoning.
